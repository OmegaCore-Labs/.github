OmegaCore Labs is an independent research organization focused on
axiomatic sovereignty, formal intelligence systems, and verifiable
mathematical construction.

Our work spans:
- Sovereign Intelligence Engine (AETHER): multi-domain causal and
  heuristic intelligence systems with auditable governance.
- Explicit resolution of Erdős Problem #142 via constructive,
  reproducible implementation of Behrend-type sets.
- Research artifacts and preprints documenting cognitive integrity,
  AI sovereignty, and system-level failure modes.

All repositories emphasize reproducibility, formal verification,
and long-horizon safety over benchmark optimization.
